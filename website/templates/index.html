About Us



About Kumpares

Kumpares began in Cebu under the leadership of CEO Christian Bautista serving merely basic meals like tapsilog. With consistency and truth to its service, the business has fully grown into making the first time unique lechon manok that comes with an egg, liver, and gizzard. Currently, the branches in Poblacion and San Pedro are owned by Mr. Michael B. Rayco and managed by Mrs. Jennifer Sinamban. Additionally, 8 more branches are expected to open its services soon.

Kumpares Mission

Kumpares PH aims to provide an amazing, and fun experience to their valued customers by satisfying their cravings with a unique style roasted chicken, pork, beef and other filipino specialties. Kumpares PH is here to serve food in a warm and friendly manner.

Kumpares Vision

Kumpares PH envisions to be one of the top performing franchising and trusted food business with its best and unique roasted chicken in the Philippines. Furthermore, the business aims to keep Kumpares PH set its standard goals high at all times.
